Depending on each Spending Score, customers were distributed into 5 categroies:
1. Window Shoppers: Those wwith a spending score less than 10

2. Budget-Conscious: Spending Score between 10 and 25

3. Normal Spenders: Spending Score between 25 and 50


4. Big Spenders: Spending Score between 50 and 75

and lastly
5. Extravagant Spenders: Customers with a spending score higher than 75
